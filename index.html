A Chronicle of Business Ventures
and Organizational Impact

Explore the key experiences and achievements that have shaped my career across diverse businesses and organizations.

Tandava Krishna Agencies

Managing Partner 1994 - Present

Established more than 2 decades ago in Vijayawada in the 1990’s, Tandava Krishna Agencies (TK Agencies) is a leading & premier provider of electric PVC pipes, tiles, sanitary-ware, and construction materials, offering renowned brands such as Finolex, Kajaria, and Cera.

BNI

Member 2016 - Present

BNI Guntur is part of a global network of business referrals with over 300,000 members in 79 countries, driving $20.01 billion USD in revenue through 14 million referrals. We have successfully completed two years of providing exceptional training and networking opportunities designed to accelerate business growth in the BNI Guntur region.

BNI

Exicutive Director 2022 - Present

At BNI Guntur, we had a vast global network of business referrals, which includes members from across 79 countries and has generated $20.01 billion USD through 14 million referrals. Over the past two years, BNI Guntur has delivered outstanding training and networking opportunities, aimed at accelerating business growth specifically within the Guntur region.

Sikhara Infra Nirmaan

Chairman & Managing Director 2022 - Present

Sikhara Infra Nirmaan Private Limited, operating under the esteemed brand 'NIRMAAN,' is a premier provider of building and construction materials and services, with experience over 25 years, based in Vijayawada. We revolutionize the way branded construction products reach customers in semi-urban and rural areas, offering homeowners direct access to high-quality materials, expert knowledge, and comprehensive services, including financing options.

Action Coach

Coach 2023 - Present

Founded by Brad Sugars in 1993, Action COACH has emerged as a top-tier business coaching franchise, celebrated for its innovation and leadership in the field. For over twenty years, it has set unparalleled standards in business coaching excellence. With more than 100 coaches across India, Action COACH continues to drive transformative success and growth.

Sikhara Welth Saviors

Partner 2023 - Present

Sikhara Wealth Saviors excels in delivering top-tier wealth management and financial planning services. We offer tailored advice to individuals and businesses, focusing on retirement planning, growth strategies, and preserving wealth across generations.

Sarayu Eco Solutions

Designated Partner 2023 - Present

Based in Vijayawada, Sarayu Eco Solutions LLP, established in January, 2020, offers cutting-edge services in the Electricity, Gas, and Water sectors. We are dedicated to pioneering sustainable solutions in energy and water management while championing community development and environmental responsibility.

Rotary Club of Vijayawada

Vice President 2023 - Present

The Rotary Club of Vijayawada Midtown, a distinguished member of Rotary International, is committed to a wide range of humanitarian initiatives including health camps, education, and environmental sustainability. With a mission to make a meaningful impact both locally and globally, this club stands out as the largest Rotary chapter in the world, boasting over 1,000 dedicated members.
